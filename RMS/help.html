🆘 OmniMart360 Help

📋 Getting Started

  • Login with your credentials
  • Explore the dashboard with 10 interactive panels
  • Hover effects and color themes guide your workflow

🔄 Backup & Restore

  • Upload your .bak file to Google Drive or Dropbox
  • Paste the shareable link or browse locally
  • Click Restore Now to recover your data

📊 Analytics & Reports

  • View sales trends and top-selling items
  • Export reports in PDF or Excel

⚙️ Troubleshooting

  • Restore failed? Check your .bak file and cloud link
  • UI issues? Restart the app or check for updates
